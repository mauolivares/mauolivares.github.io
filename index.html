I am a postdoct at the Statistics and Econometrics Group in the Statistics Department, Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität (LMU) Munich.

I received a Ph.D. in Economics from the University of Illinois Urbana Champaign. Before joining LMU, I had the pleasure of being a postdoctoral research fellow at University College London.

My primary research interests are in Econometrics. In particular, I study a class of inference methods to deal with semi-parametric and nonparametric hypotheses: permutation/randomization tests.
